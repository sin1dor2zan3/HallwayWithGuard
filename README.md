### Gameplay Prototype:

Shows off player movement, enemy movement, bouncing on mushrooms, and collisions with obstacles, enemies to lose, and the macguffin to win.



### Artstyle Prototype:

Shows off the player, enemy, and macguffin models. Also shows off level design and UI.





### Instructions:



W - Forward

A - Left

S - Backward

D - Right

Space - Jump

Mouse - Look
